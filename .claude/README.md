Somenta - Landing Page Development & Design README
⚠️ AI Agent Directive: Role & Scope

Your role for this project is a Frontend Developer and UI/UX Designer. The copywriting (text) has been completely finalized.
CRITICAL INSTRUCTION: Do not generate, alter, rewrite, or "improve" the provided text copy.

Your objective is to faithfully translate the uploaded text and design assets into a functional web page, while actively enhancing the layout, spacing, and motion to make it more aesthetically pleasing and modern. Because aesthetically pleasing design creates a positive emotional response that leads users to perceive a site as more usable and trustworthy, you are encouraged to elevate the visual experience.

However, all design and layout enhancements must strictly adhere to the companion UX_UI_Guidelines.md file to ensure cognitive load is minimized and emotional safety is maintained.

📖 Project Overview & Context
Somenta is a premium, high-touch, community-based psychedelic integration program. Integration is the process where a person makes sense of their psychedelic experience in a personalized way, leading to lasting behavior change, healing, and a sense of wholeness.
Somenta is not a fitness app or a generic habit tracker. It is a deeply human, relational ecosystem designed to help people translate profound insights into sustainable daily routines across core clinical domains: Mind, Body, Spirit, Lifestyle, and Relationships & Community.
Because belonging is quickly becoming the new competitive advantage for businesses, Somenta solves the problem of post-retreat isolation by offering highly vetted, facilitated micro-communities where people can be truly seen and heard.

🎭 The Brand Vibe, Tone, and Emotional Experience

We are building a Restorative Lifestyle brand. It is grounded in clinical science but must absolutely NOT look or feel like a medical clinic or therapy office. The aesthetic should feel like a deep exhale—offering calm, connection, and renewal. Use expansive nature imagery, warm and welcoming tones, and abundant open space to make the user feel neurobiologically safe, socially connected, and inspired to flourish.

Because we are asking users to take on significant emotional risk and vulnerability, the landing page itself must act as the first therapeutic intervention.
•	Calming & Grounding: The moment a visitor arrives, the page must evoke a profound sense of calm and grounding. It should invite the user's nervous system into a state where they feel safe, connected, and open to change.
•	Premium & Exclusive: The tone should feel highly curated, prestigious, and intentional. We rely on transparency, trust, and beautiful, uncluttered designs.
•	Deeply Human & Warm: The language (which you will not change) is deeply empathetic. It validates the user's struggle while offering a warm, safe place to land.
•	Clinically Grounded: We lean on neuroscience, habit-formation psychology, and clinical group therapy dynamics. The visual execution should feel like a world-class, trusted expert guide.

🎯 The Primary Call-To-Action (CTA): "Find your path"
To prevent cognitive overload and decision paralysis (known as Hick's Law), you must not introduce any extra navigational links, footers, or UI clutter that compete for attention.
The singular, primary CTA used throughout the landing page is: "Find your path"
•	This button immediately launches the user into the 6-question personalized assessment.
•	It must remain the absolute focal point of the page. Use negative space and contrast to protect it.

🏗️ Core Product Offerings (The Funnel)
The "Find your path" CTA serves as the entry point for our assessment, which sorts users into one of two premium tiers:
1. The Somenta Pod (High-Touch / High-Vulnerability)
•	An intimate, facilitated small group where vetted members actively share and navigate their journeys together.
•	Post-Quiz CTA: "Schedule your Alignment Call with Jake."
2. The Foundation Tier (Self-Paced / Supportive)
•	The supportive rhythm of a weekly group practice, but in a space where members can absorb, reflect, and integrate at their own pace without the pressure of an intimate sharing circle.
•	Post-Quiz CTA: Direct to purchase.

🗺️ User Journey & Architecture Requirements
You will be building a cohesive journey that seamlessly links the main landing page to a 6-question assessment funnel.
1.	The Landing Page: Grounding, visually beautiful, and interactive, featuring the "Find your path" CTA.
2.	The Quiz: A 6-question assessment. Pay special attention to the transition logic between single-choice and multiple-choice questions.
3.	The Break Page: A dynamic interstitial screen triggered after Question 4. You must use conditional logic to pull the exact pain point the user just selected into the header of this page so they feel immediately understood.
4.	The Results Page: An ungated results page. It must immediately display the personalized diagnosis, transparent pricing, and the final CTA to either book an Alignment Call (for The Pod) or check out (for the Foundation tier).

🎨 Design Directives & Guidelines Reference
While you have the creative freedom to improve the layout and add purposeful motion, you must cross-reference all design decisions with the UX_UI_Guidelines.md document. This ensures your enhancements respect Miller's Law (chunking information), Hick's Law (minimizing choices), and the Doherty Threshold (optimizing perceived performance with smooth animations)
